Who I am

As I am writing this I'm currently enjoying a week-long break from school after (most of) the midterms, so I'll use this time to do as much of the groundwork for this site as possible so it will not take too much commitment to maintain when I have less free time.

My music taste is mostly Japan-oriented and prominently electronic, with j-pop(including but not limited to vocaloid), artcore, trance, and some high energy ear-shattering sounds like j-core and hardstyle. My favorite musician is Sakuzyo.

I enjoy indie games, multiplayer games which focus on the social aspect such as ff14 and (very recently) webfishing, and as of now I have been hopelessly addicted to rhythm games for a year: I play Arcaea on mobile, and Gekichumai at the arcade. I also play Synth riders on my VR sometimes.
